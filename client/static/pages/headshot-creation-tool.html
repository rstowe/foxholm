Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI headshots

What kind of photos work best?

Any clear photo where your face is visible works great. The AI performs best with well-lit photos where you're facing the camera, but it can work with profile shots and various lighting conditions.

How long does it take?

Most headshots are ready in 20-30 seconds. During peak times, it might take up to a minute, but you'll never wait more than that for your professional portrait.

Can I use these commercially?

Yes! All headshots come with a full commercial license. Use them on LinkedIn, websites, business cards, or anywhere else you need a professional image.

Will it still look like me?

Absolutely. Our AI enhances your natural features and improves lighting and backgrounds while maintaining your authentic appearance. You'll look like yourself on your best day.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your headshot, our support team will work with you to make it right or provide a refund.

Do you store my photos?

Your privacy is our priority. We process your photos in real-time and don't store any images after delivery unless you explicitly save them to your account.
