Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about photo restoration

What photo conditions can you restore?

We can restore photos with tears, water damage, fading, scratches, stains, and missing pieces. Even severely damaged photos often have remarkable results.

Do I need the original photo?

No! A good quality scan or even a phone photo of the damaged print works perfectly. Just ensure the photo is well-lit and in focus.

How long does restoration take?

Most photos are restored in 30-60 seconds. Complex restorations might take up to 2 minutes, but you'll never wait long for results.

Will it change how people look?

No. Our AI is trained to restore, not alter. It maintains facial features and characteristics while repairing damage.

Can you restore color to B&W photos?

Yes! We offer intelligent colorization that adds historically accurate colors to black and white photos.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If the restoration doesn't meet expectations, our team will help or provide a refund.
