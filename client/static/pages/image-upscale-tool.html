Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI upscaling

How much can I upscale an image?

You can upscale images up to 4x their original dimensions. A 500x500 image becomes 2000x2000. The AI maintains quality throughout the process.

What file types are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output. You can convert between formats during the upscaling process.

Is there a file size limit?

Input images can be up to 10MB and 2048x2048 pixels. Output images can reach 8192x8192 pixels depending on the upscale factor.

How is this different from Photoshop?

Traditional tools use interpolation which creates blurry results. Our AI understands image content and generates new, realistic detail that didn't exist in the original.

Can I process multiple images?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include batch processing. Upload multiple images and process them all with the same settings.

Do you store my images?

No. Images are processed in real-time and immediately deleted from our servers after you download the result. Your privacy is guaranteed.
